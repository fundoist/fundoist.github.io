GST Rental Invoice

Simple GST invoicing for Indian landlords and property managers.

← Fundoist

What it does

A mobile app for generating GST-compliant monthly rent invoices. One tap produces invoices for all your tenants, with CGST and SGST calculated automatically and PDFs saved directly to your Google Drive.

Built for landlords and property managers handling multiple properties, tenants, or entities.

Features

  • One-tap invoices. Generate GST-compliant invoices for all tenants at once.
  • Auto GST calculation. 9% CGST + 9% SGST applied automatically.
  • PDF to Drive. Invoices saved directly to your Google Drive.
  • Multi-property. Manage multiple landlords, properties, and tenants in one place.

Your data, your control

All data stays in your own Google account. There are no Fundoist servers holding your spreadsheets or PDFs — everything lives in your Drive, under your control. You can revoke access anytime from Google account settings.

See the privacy policy for details.

How it works

  1. Sign in with your Google account.
  2. Setup creates a spreadsheet and folder in your Drive.
  3. Add your properties and tenants.
  4. Generate invoices monthly with one tap.
