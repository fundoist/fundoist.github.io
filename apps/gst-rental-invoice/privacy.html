Overview

GST Rental Invoice ("the app") is a mobile application that helps users generate and manage GST invoices. This policy explains how the app handles your information.

Information the app accesses

Google account information

When you sign in with Google, the app reads:

  • Your email address (for display)
  • Your name (for display)
  • Your profile picture (for display)

Google services data

With your explicit permission, the app accesses:

  • Google Sheets — to store and retrieve invoice data, tenant records, landlord details, property information, and rent configurations.
  • Google Drive — to store generated PDF invoices in your personal Drive.

How your information is used

  • Authentication — to verify your identity and grant access to the app.
  • Data storage — all your business data (invoices, tenants, landlords, properties, rents) is stored in your own Google Sheets and Drive, not on any Fundoist server.
  • Invoice generation — to create PDF invoices and save them to your Drive.

Data storage and security

Your data stays with you. Fundoist does not operate servers that store your business data. All information is stored in your personal Google Sheets spreadsheet and your personal Google Drive folder.

The app accesses this data only while you are actively using it, via Google's OAuth 2.0 authentication.

Third-party services

  • Google Sign-In for authentication — Google Privacy Policy.
  • Google Sheets API for data storage — Google API Services User Data Policy.
  • Google Drive API for PDF storage — Google API Services User Data Policy.

Data sharing

The app does not:

  • Sell your personal information
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Use your data for advertising
  • Store your data on any Fundoist server
  • Access your data when you are not using the app

Your rights

  • Revoke access anytime via Google Account Permissions.
  • Delete your data by deleting the Google Sheet and Drive folder created by the app.
  • Export your data directly from Google Sheets and Drive.

Data retention

Since all data is stored in your Google account, you control retention. Fundoist does not retain any of your data independently.

Children's privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13. Fundoist does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated periodically. Changes will be reflected in the "last updated" date at the top.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Use the support form or email hello@fundoist.com.

Google API Services disclosure

GST Rental Invoice's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
